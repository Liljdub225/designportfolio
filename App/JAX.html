JAX INC.

During my time as the graphic design intern at JAX, I collaborated with the marketing and technical teams to develop different projects. We created templates, changed visuals, and updated technical information. Explore this page to see some of the projects that we completed.

Industry Sheets

These sheets were intended to inform our clients in each industry what product would work best for them in their equipment. I communicated with the marketing department to receive the most updated information for each industry. To ensure accuracy, I presented them to the technical department and made any revisions they requested. Once finalized, they were printed and sent to the sales team to deliver to their customers.

Miscellaneous Sheets

The purpose of these sheets was based on the demand from the field. When the sales team needed literature, the marketing and technical teams would collaborate and create something that could be utilized out in the field when interacting with the customers.
