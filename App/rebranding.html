The Ridge Community Church Rebranding Project

I completed this rebranding project for The Ridge Community Church as part of an identity design course. The Ridge's mission is to bring the hope of Jesus into every home through initiatives like Ridge Groups and Alpha, which guide new believers and foster community connections. With this mission in mind, I aimed to design a mark that reflects their welcoming spirit and commitment to serving others. The concept of open doors became the focus of the logo, representing belonging and approachability for anyone stepping into The Ridge for the first time.


Color played a central role as well. I chose orange and blue to represent individual Christians coming together in worship, with green—formed when the two blend—symbolizing the unity that emerges from this gathering. This project challenged me to capture the heart of an organization through visual identity while preserving its core values, helping me grow in creating thoughtful and authentic brand systems.
