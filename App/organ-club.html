ORGAN CLUB FLYERS

Attending Concordia University of Wisconsin offered me many opportunities to grow. Being involved in Organ Club was one of those that I took advantage of. This club hosted monthly events to engage with the student population and increase appreciation of the music world. My role in the club was to create promotional posters for these events and present them to the board, making changes along the way.
