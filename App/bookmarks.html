Custom Bookmarks

This was a personal project I created to show appreciation for my friends. I asked each of them for their favorite Bible verse and then designed a custom bookmark featuring their verse and name. Each design reflects not only the verse but also the personality of the individual.


For example, the first bookmark was for my friend Allis, who is incredibly sweet. I chose lightweight typography, a tan color to reflect her comforting nature, and a floral image. Together, these elements represent her warmth and her ability to make others smile and feel cared for. Overall, the bookmarks were successful in capturing each friend's character in a meaningful, thoughtful way.
